A dog in Japan called, "Jason" a.k.a. Micheal J. Hero, having superpowers, Jason witnessed the cruelty of the outside world’s specimens and thus deciding vengeance upon them by then riding a spaceship and using his intellect to fight for and save the world. You have two stages and bonus stages (coming soon) in the game. First stage is a Rhythm game. Second stage is a Shooting game.
